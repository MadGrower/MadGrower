Who are we?

Our server allows you to join from version 1.8.x to 1.20.x. You can enjoy CityBuild and manage your own farms. In addition to CityBuild, we offer game modes like SkyBlock and BedWars, with more planned. Our founding team consists of Bryan, who leads the server, and Marcel, our host and partner. Together, we aim to build a vibrant community and provide players with lots of fun on the server. We look forward to welcoming you and experiencing unforgettable Minecraft adventures together!

CityBuild

CityBuild is a Minecraft game mode where players can build their own cities and structures. They can purchase plots, construct buildings, gather resources, and trade to develop and expand their city.

  • Money system
  • Farming world
  • Private plots
  • and more...

SkyBlock

SkyBlock is a challenging Minecraft game mode where players start on a small floating island with limited resources. They must be creative to gather materials, expand their island, and build a survival network in a very limited space.

  • Money system
  • Private island
  • Survival
  • and more...

Why vote?

Voting in Minecraft allows players to support their favorite servers by voting daily. This helps servers gain popularity, bringing in more players, building a more active community, and improving the overall experience.
